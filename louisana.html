IMMERSIVE
GALLERY

Louisiana needed a creative concept to create a digital gallery for their guests to get an immersive experience of their massive collection. My group and I came up with an idea in the form of a gallery containing rooms, that the viewer could visit depending on their mood, achieving a similar feeling to how you would normally visit the museum. In this project, I worked with idea generation, concept development, wireframing, and coding the website.
